## About Our Team

The ACADEX team is composed of dedicated individuals committed to creating an efficient and user-friendly Outcome Based Grading System. Our diverse skill set spans full-stack development, UI/UX design, quality assurance, database management, and technical documentation.

### Our Mission

To develop a comprehensive academic grading system that simplifies the outcome-based education approach for educational institutions, making grade computation and assessment tracking seamless and accurate.
